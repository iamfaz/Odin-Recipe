Buttermilk Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 g Chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp Curry powder
  • 1 tsp Maggi seasoning sauce or soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup corn flour
  • 1/2 cup rice flour

Buttermilk Sauce

  • 40 g salted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 sprigs curry leaves
  • 2 fresh chili padi (bird's eye chili)
  • 1 1/2 cup evaporated milk (up to 2 cups)
  • 2 tbsp condensed milk or honey
  • salt and pepper (to season)
  • 2 tbsp water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken to equal pieces.
  2. Marinade with curry powder, white pepper and salt and Maggi seasoning sauce (or soy sauce. Add 1 egg and mix.
  3. Prepare batter. Mix equal portion of rice flour and corn flour. Dip chicken into flour until coated evenly on all sides and dust off any excess flour.
  4. Fry chicken. Heat enough oil in a deep pan and fry chicken in batches, for about 3-4 minutes or until golden brown on all sides. Be sure not to overcrowd the pan as it will not crisp up and turn soggy. Dish out the fried chicken pieces and drain on a paper towel.
  5. Using a seperate pan on medium high heat, fry butter, chopped garlic and chili padi for 1 minute. Remove curry leaves from stalk and add into pan. Add in salt and pepper.
  6. Add in evaporated milk and condensed milk and let it come to a simmer for 1 minute. Add 2 tbsp water if you want to make it saucier.
  7. Add in fried chicken pieces and toss chicken so it coats evenly with sauce. The sauce will be thick as the dish cools. Dish out and serve on rice.
